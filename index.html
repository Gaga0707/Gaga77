PERSONAL PROJECTS

Design and implementation of SegNet image semantic segmentation network

[Report]

Based on the FCN network architecture, the decoding part of it is optimised. SegNet uses pooling index passing, and the decoder performs nonlinear up-sampling using pooling indices computed in the maximum pooling step of the corresponding encoder, which enables better recovery of the spatial information and obtains finer segmentation results, which is more desirable when dealing with complex images or tasks that require high-precision segmentation.

TEAM PROJECTS

The Power Curve of Cyclists Based on the Differential Equation Model

A large amount of power data was analyzed by fitting and merging regressions to simulate the rider's power output and thresholds over different time periods. Differential equation models were used to predict the value of power that an athlete should use at the appropriate position in a race. Additionally, I completed a sensitivity analysis of the model to explore the robustness of the model to certain parameters.

I was responsible for research data processing, analysis and prediction using mathematical models.

Mowing Pioneer - Unmanned operation using SLAM visual mapping and UWB positioning technology

This is an internet+ project, the group has 7 members, we plan to make a multi-functional integrated intelligent lawn mower, including water and nutrient monitoring, pest monitoring and other functions.

I was mainly responsible for the design of intelligent path recognition and planning, local coverage by means of Boustrophedon decomposition, backtracking path planning by using advanced A* algorithms, the processor automatically plans a reasonable and barrier-free driving path at the processor, and analyzes the working environment step by step in a detailed and in-depth manner.
